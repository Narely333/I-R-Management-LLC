Who We Are

Built by hand.
Run by family.

I&R Management LLC is owned and operated by Israel and Raquel Rivas. What started as hands-on remodeling work has grown into a full general contracting company — but the approach hasn't changed: show up, do the work right, and leave every home better than we found it.

Over the years we've taken on full house flips down to the studs, kitchen and bathroom remodels, custom porches and decks, and just about everything in between. Every project on this site is real work we've completed.
